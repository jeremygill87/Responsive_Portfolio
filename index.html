About Me

My name is Jeremy, but I generally just go by my last name: Gill. There's just something about one-syllable names that I find alluring. I am based in New York City, but have deep ties to Cleveland, OH, where my fondest memories were made. Though I am a high school English teacher, I have always been a huge fan of technology but never really considered a career in tech until I moved to New York and met one of the animators for the Harry Potter movie series. When the man who designed and animated the knife that killed Dobby tells you that you should pursue a career you are passionate about, you should listen. So here I am. Ready to code.
